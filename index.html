Our Services

We provide integrated environmental consulting services designed to support regulatory compliance, environmental protection, and sustainable development.

Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA)

Preparation of Environmental Impact Assessment studies for development, infrastructure, and industrial projects.

Environmental Audits

Conducting environmental audits to assess compliance with applicable environmental regulations and standards.

Environmental Management Plans (EMP)

Development of environmental management and monitoring plans for construction and operational phases.

Environmental Licensing Support

Assistance with obtaining environmental permits and licenses for construction and operation.

Contaminated Site Assessment & Treatment

Preparation of remediation, rehabilitation, and site closure plans for contaminated areas.

Waste Management Solutions

Environmentally responsible waste collection and transportation solutions, including hazardous and medical waste.

Environmental Compliance Services

Supporting organizations in meeting local and international environmental regulations.

Training & Environmental Awareness

Delivery of training programs, workshops, and awareness campaigns for institutions and individuals.
